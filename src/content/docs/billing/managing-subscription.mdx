## Where billing lives

In the app: **Settings → Billing**. Only Owners and Admins see this section.

You'll find:

- Your current plan
- Trial status (if you're still in the 14-day trial)
- One of two buttons that opens Stripe's hosted Customer Portal in a new tab:
  - **Manage Subscription** — shown when you have an active subscription.
  - **Subscription Portal** — shown after a plan change or in certain account states.

Both buttons go to the same place. Most actual billing operations — updating your card, changing plans, downloading invoices, canceling — happen in the Stripe Customer Portal rather than in our UI directly. We pass you through to Stripe and let their hosted UX handle the payment-card-handling parts. Stripe is PCI-compliant by default; we never see your card data.

## Starting a subscription (first time)

After your 14-day trial ends (or earlier if you're ready to subscribe):

1. **Settings → Billing**.
2. Click **Choose a plan** (or **Upgrade** if you're already past the trial limit).
3. You're redirected to a **Stripe Checkout** session — a hosted page with the plan selection and payment fields.
4. Enter your card details and confirm.
5. You're returned to **Settings → Billing** with the success state.

The workspace is now on a paid plan. The first charge happens immediately for the prorated amount.

## Updating your payment method

1. **Settings → Billing → Manage Subscription**.
2. Stripe Customer Portal opens.
3. Under **Payment methods**, click **Add payment method** or update the existing card.
4. Save.

The new card applies to your next charge. The current cycle's payment is unchanged.

## Changing plans

Same path — through the Stripe Customer Portal:

1. **Settings → Billing → Manage Subscription**.
2. Click **Update plan** (or similar; Stripe's UI evolves).
3. Pick the new tier.
4. Stripe walks you through the confirmation, prorations, and (if applicable) the new charge.

For upgrades, the change usually takes effect immediately and you're charged the prorated difference.

For downgrades, the change applies at the end of the current billing cycle (you don't lose features you've paid for).

> **First-time subscriptions** route through Stripe Checkout (a session-based flow). **Subsequent plan changes** route through the Customer Portal's update-confirm flow. Same end result, slightly different UX.

## What you can do in the Customer Portal

The Customer Portal handles:

- **Payment methods** — add, remove, set default
- **Billing address** — used on invoices
- **Tax ID** — for VAT-registered businesses, set your tax ID so it appears on invoices
- **Invoices** — list, download as PDF, email to yourself
- **Cancel subscription** — at end of current cycle

When you come back to SendBriefs after a change, the workspace reflects the new state (typically within seconds; up to a minute for some changes via webhook).

## Billing cycle and renewal

- **Monthly plans** charge on the same day each month (closest business day if your billing day is on a weekend).
- **Annual plans** charge once a year on the anniversary of your subscription start.

You'll get an email from Stripe with the receipt after each charge. SendBriefs surfaces the receipt in the Customer Portal too.

## Failed payments

If your card is declined:

1. Stripe retries on its standard schedule (typically 3 / 5 / 7 days).
2. You get email notifications from Stripe for each retry.
3. SendBriefs surfaces an in-app banner once a payment fails.

If retries don't succeed within Stripe's window, **the workspace transitions to a suspended state**. Briefs stop sending; data is preserved. Update your payment in the Customer Portal and the workspace resumes once the next charge succeeds.

## Multi-workspace billing

Each workspace has its own subscription. No "umbrella" billing across multiple workspaces in the current preview.

If you operate multiple workspaces and need a consolidated invoice, that's an Enterprise conversation — [email support](mailto:support@sendbriefs.com).

## What's not in the product today

To be honest about gaps:

- **ACH / wire / SEPA payments.** Only credit card / debit card via Stripe Checkout in the preview. Larger plans may add ACH later.
- **Tax-info edits inside the SendBriefs UI.** Tax ID and billing address live in the Stripe Customer Portal; we don't mirror them in our own settings.
- **Multi-currency.** USD by default in the preview. We'll expand currency options as we ship to more regions.
- **Reseller / partner pricing.** Not currently structured. If you're managing workspaces for multiple agency clients, email support to discuss.

## Common gotchas

- **Plan changes go through Stripe.** If you click "Update plan" in our UI and nothing happens, check whether your browser's pop-up blocker is preventing the Stripe Portal from opening.
- **Tax ID lives in Stripe.** To put a VAT number or business tax ID on your invoices, set it in the Customer Portal (not in our settings — that's not where Stripe pulls it from).
- **You can't have two cards on file.** The Customer Portal allows multiple payment methods but only one is the "default." Updating the card replaces the default.
- **Owners and Admins only.** Members can't see billing. The "Manage Subscription" button is hidden for them.
