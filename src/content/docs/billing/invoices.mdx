## Where invoices live

Stripe handles all billing, including invoice generation and storage. To access invoices:

1. In SendBriefs: **Settings → Billing**.
2. Click **Manage in the customer portal**.
3. The Stripe Customer Portal opens in a new tab.
4. Click **Invoices** in the portal navigation.

You'll see a table of every invoice with:

- Invoice number
- Date issued
- Amount
- Status (paid, open, past due, etc.)
- A **Download** link for the PDF

## Downloading invoices

In the Customer Portal, click the PDF icon next to any invoice. The PDF downloads immediately.

The PDF includes:

- Your business name and billing address (from the address you've set in the Customer Portal)
- SendBriefs business name and address
- Invoice number and date
- Period covered
- Line items
- Subtotal, tax (if applicable), total
- Payment method (last 4 digits of card)
- Payment date

The PDF format is the standard Stripe invoice and is suitable for expense systems (Expensify, Brex, Ramp, Concur, etc.).

## Getting invoices to accounting

A few patterns:

### Option A: forward from your inbox

After each charge, Stripe emails the workspace billing contact a receipt with the invoice attached. Forward that email to your accountant or expense system's email-in address. Most expense tools have one.

### Option B: download as a batch at tax time

In the Stripe Customer Portal's Invoices tab, you can download invoices one at a time. For tax season, set aside 5 minutes to grab them all and ZIP them yourself.

### Option C: Stripe receipt emails

Stripe sends an auto-receipt email after every successful charge. If your accountant just needs "did the agency get paid for these months," forwarding the receipts (rather than the invoices) is sometimes lighter.

## Tax ID on invoices

If your business needs invoices to show a tax ID (VAT number, GST number, US sales tax registration):

1. Open the Stripe Customer Portal.
2. Under **Billing information**, add your **Tax ID**.
3. Save.

The tax ID appears on the next invoice. To re-issue past invoices with the new tax info, contact support — Stripe doesn't change historical invoices automatically.

### EU VAT specifics

If you're EU-based with a valid VAT number, Stripe applies the reverse-charge mechanism (VAT 0%, you self-account). Stripe validates the VAT number with VIES when you enter it; you'll see the validation status in the Customer Portal.

### US state sales tax

Sales tax handling depends on your billing address and the jurisdictions SendBriefs is required to collect in. The Stripe Customer Portal shows the tax line on each invoice when applicable.

## Re-issuing invoices

To re-issue an invoice (e.g., your billing address changed and you need a clean version):

1. Update the relevant info in the Stripe Customer Portal.
2. Email support — Stripe's flow for re-issuing historical invoices requires us to nudge it manually.
3. We'll send the re-issued PDF within 1 business day.

## Receipts vs invoices

For credit card payments, the invoice + the payment confirmation in the PDF together serve as your receipt. The Stripe Customer Portal also has separate **Receipts** that download as PDFs if your accounting system specifically requires "receipt" vs "invoice."

## Currency

Invoices are issued in USD by default in the current preview. To request a different currency, talk to support — currency choice is set on the Stripe subscription and can't be changed mid-cycle.

## Refunds

If a refund is issued (e.g., you cancel within the refund window of an annual plan), the refund appears as a credit on your next invoice — or, for full refunds, as a separate **Credit note** PDF in the Customer Portal.

## Tax-time bundle

A common ask: "I need all my SendBriefs invoices from last year for filing."

The Stripe Customer Portal's Invoices tab has date filters. Set it to your fiscal year and download each PDF one at a time, or email support and we can produce a bundled ZIP within a business day.

## What's not in the product today

- **In-app invoice list.** All invoice access goes through the Stripe Customer Portal — we don't mirror the list inside SendBriefs.
- **Auto-forward invoices to a specific email.** Stripe sends to the workspace's billing-contact email. There's no built-in "also send invoices to accounting@" toggle yet. The manual path is to set a forwarding rule in your inbox.
- **Native expense-system integrations.** No direct push to Expensify/Brex/Ramp. The PDF format works with all of them via forward-to-inbox.

## Common gotchas

- **Billing email vs workspace owner email.** Stripe sends invoice emails to the billing contact configured in the Customer Portal — which may differ from the workspace Owner if you set it that way. Check the Customer Portal's contact settings if invoices are going to the wrong address.
- **PDF attachment size.** Some corporate email systems strip attachments over a threshold. If your accountant says "the invoice didn't come through," download from the Customer Portal directly.
- **Tax info changes are forward-only.** Updating your tax ID applies to future invoices. To update past ones, contact support.
