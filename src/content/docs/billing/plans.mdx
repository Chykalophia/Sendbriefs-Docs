> **Heads up: SendBriefs is in Founders Circle preview.** Plan amounts and limits below describe the current preview tiers — they may change before general availability. Treat this page as the *shape* of the plans, not contractual pricing.
>
> **App naming vs marketing naming.** The product stores plan tiers under internal keys (`free`, `trial`, `starter`, `pro`, `professional`, `business`, `enterprise`). The marketing-page names — **Solo / Agency / Agency Pro / Enterprise** — are the customer-facing labels. Both refer to the same subscription system; the docs use the marketing names because that's what you'll see on the pricing page and on invoices.

## How to pick a plan

Pick based on the **constraint that bites first**:

- If you'll exceed the **client count** in 6 months, size up.
- If you'll exceed the **team size** in 6 months, size up.
- If you need a feature only at higher tiers (e.g., API access, SSO), pick the tier that includes it.
- If none of the above, start one tier lower and upgrade later — upgrades are immediate and prorated.

## Plan summary

| Plan | Featured | Position |
|---|---|---|
| **Solo** | — | Founders / consultants with a small roster |
| **Agency** | Yes — featured | Most agencies start here |
| **Agency Pro** | — | Multi-line agencies |
| **Enterprise** | — | Custom contract, custom integrations, SLA |

> Specific client / team / send caps per plan change as we tune the product. **See [sendbriefs.com/pricing](https://sendbriefs.com/pricing) for current limits** — we don't quote numeric caps here so this page stays accurate as the plans evolve.

All plans include:

- The full block editor and brief state machine
- Workspace + member roles (Owner / Admin / Member)
- The client portal (token-based, branded)
- Email delivery via SendGrid
- In-app + email notifications
- Available integrations (Google, Slack, ClickUp)
- 14-day free trial on signup

What changes across tiers (as currently scoped):

- **Number of clients** allowed in the workspace
- **Number of team members** allowed in the workspace
- **API access** (Enterprise only, when it ships — see [API access](/api/coming-soon/))

**Add-ons (purchased separately):**

- **White-label** — suppresses "Sent via SendBriefs" branding from emails and the portal.
- **Custom domain** — host the recipient portal on your own subdomain (e.g., `briefs.youragency.com`).

Both are bundled into the **Enterprise** tier by default; available as à la carte purchases on paid plans below Enterprise. Manage from **Settings → Billing** under **Add-ons**.

## What's included regardless of plan

A few things don't depend on tier:

- **Unlimited briefs.** No per-send fee. If your workspace is on the Agency plan with 25 clients, you can send weekly briefs to all 25 without extra charge.
- **All integrations live for your account.** New integrations don't gate to higher tiers as we ship them.
- **All starter templates.** New templates we ship are available to everyone.
- **The 14-day trial** has full feature access at the Agency tier's limits — generous enough to test real workflows.

## Free trial

Every signup includes a **14-day free trial**, no credit card required. During the trial:

- Trial-tier client and user limits apply (see [sendbriefs.com/pricing](https://sendbriefs.com/pricing) for current numbers).
- All features are available, with a few Pro-gated surfaces (e.g., the `verified` portal access tier) locked.
- Briefs send normally.

At day 15, you pick a plan and add payment, or your workspace pauses. **Your data stays intact** during the pause — you just can't send new briefs until you pick a plan.

## Annual vs monthly billing

Annual plans typically save vs. monthly (we don't hardcode the exact discount here — see the pricing page). Annual is paid upfront in one charge.

Once you're confident SendBriefs is the platform you'll stay on, annual is the right call. The savings compound over the contract length, and you only have one renewal date to track.

## Enterprise

Custom contracts. Volume pricing. Compliance support (SOC 2 reports, DPAs, custom data residency). Dedicated customer success.

Typical Enterprise features when they're live:

- Unlimited clients and team members
- API access (when it ships)
- SSO via SAML or OIDC
- Custom data retention
- Custom SLA
- Dedicated support channel

Talk to sales: [email support@sendbriefs.com](mailto:support@sendbriefs.com) and mention "Enterprise inquiry."

## What happens at plan limits

Plan limits (client count, team size) are checked when you try to *add* a new one:

- **At the client limit**: adding another client surfaces a "you're at your plan limit" prompt with an **Upgrade** button.
- **At the team limit**: same pattern for invites.

There's no surprise charge if you hit a limit — the workspace just blocks the addition until you either upgrade or remove something.

For the metering details (what's counted, how often, when limits are checked), see [Plan limits & metering](/billing/limits-and-metering/).
