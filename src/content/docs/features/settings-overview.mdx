<aside class="sb-tldr">
<p class="sb-tldr-label">TL;DR</p>
<strong>Settings</strong> in the sidebar opens a tabbed page with 16 tabs grouped into 5 sections. The tab you can reach is gated by your role. Most tabs require <strong>settings:update</strong> (Owner or Admin). Direct-link any tab with <code>?tab=&lt;id&gt;</code>.

## Tab behavior

- **Direct link** any tab with `app.sendbriefs.com/settings?tab=<id>` â€” e.g., `?tab=email-templates` jumps straight to Email Layout.
- **Role gating** is enforced server-side; tabs hidden in the sidebar can't be reached by URL-typing into a higher-permission tab.
- **Tab IDs vs display labels** differ in places â€” `email-templates` shows as "Email Layout", `assets` shows as "Media Library", `audit` shows as "Logging", `scheduled-reports` shows as "Scheduling", `danger` shows as "Danger Zone", `email` shows as "Email Settings".

## What's NOT in Settings

A few things people expect in Settings live elsewhere:

- **/profile** â€” your personal account: name, password, your MFA, your active sessions. Sidebar avatar â†’ Profile.
- **/notifications** â€” your in-app notification center (the bell-icon view). The Settings â†’ Notifications tab is for *preferences*; the standalone page is for the *list*.
- **/activity** â€” workspace activity feed, email delivery, raw audit log. Settings â†’ Logging links here.
- **/team** â€” workspace member directory page (with per-member detail). Settings â†’ Team is the management surface; /team is the directory view.

## Permission quick reference

| Action | Required permission |
|---|---|
| View most settings tabs | `settings:read` (any member can see Notifications) |
| Edit most settings | `settings:update` (Owner or Admin) |
| Access Billing | `billing:read` (Owner or Admin) |
| Update Billing | `billing:update` (Owner or Admin) |
| Approve briefs | `reports:approve` (Owner or Admin) |
| Manage integrations | `integrations:update` (Owner or Admin) |
| Workspace deletion | `workspace:delete` (Owner only) |
