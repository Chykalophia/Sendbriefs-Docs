## What SendBriefs is

SendBriefs is a reporting platform built for agencies. Instead of rebuilding the same monthly report by hand, you:

1. **Build a brief once** in the block-based editor, save it as a template.
2. **Connect a client** and the data sources behind their report.
3. SendBriefs **renders** the brief from the template each period.
4. The brief moves through your **approval workflow** (optional) and goes out by email and/or a hosted client portal.
5. We log delivery, opens, and clicks so you have a paper trail.

You stay in the loop for review, edits, and approvals. You stop being the bottleneck.

> A note on terminology — what we call a "brief" appears in the app as **Reports** in the left sidebar. Same thing. The docs use "brief" everywhere because that's the artifact your client receives.

## What it's not

- **Not a dashboard.** Dashboards live forever and nobody reads them. Briefs have a delivery date, a recipient, and a "this is what changed" framing.
- **Not a writing tool.** SendBriefs renders briefs from templates and connected data. It doesn't draft prose for you.
- **Not a BI platform.** If you need ad-hoc analysis across 50M rows, use a warehouse. SendBriefs is the last mile — the report your client actually opens.

## Who SendBriefs is for

You'll get the most out of it if:

- You manage between **5 and 75 clients** who each get recurring reports.
- Your reporting cadence is **weekly, biweekly, monthly, or quarterly**.
- You currently spend **2+ hours per client per month** assembling reports.
- Friday afternoons get eaten by report assembly more often than you'd like.

If you only send one report a year to one client, this is overkill. If you have 5,000 SaaS-style accounts auto-generating reports, you probably need something with more dev resources behind it.
