## What SendBriefs is

SendBriefs is a reporting platform built for agencies that send 20+ client briefs per month. Instead of rebuilding the same monthly report by hand, you build a template once. Every period, SendBriefs:

1. Pulls fresh data from your connected sources (analytics, ad platforms, CRM, billing, etc.)
2. Renders the brief from your template with the new data
3. Sends it to the client on the schedule you set
4. Logs delivery, opens, and approvals so you have a paper trail

You stay in the loop for review, approvals, and edits — but you stop being the bottleneck.

## What it's not

- **Not a dashboard.** Dashboards live forever and nobody reads them. Briefs have a delivery date, a recipient, and a "this is what changed" framing.
- **Not a writing tool.** SendBriefs renders briefs from templates and data. It doesn't draft prose for you (you can plug in AI yourself if you want).
- **Not a BI platform.** If you need ad-hoc analysis across 50M rows, use a warehouse. SendBriefs is for the last mile — the report your client actually opens.

## Who SendBriefs is for

You'll get the most out of it if:

- You manage between **5 and 500 clients** who each get recurring reports.
- Your reporting cadence is **weekly or monthly** (annual is supported but not the sweet spot).
- You currently spend **2+ hours per client per month** assembling reports.
- Friday afternoons get eaten by report assembly more often than you'd like.

If you only send one report a year to one client, this is overkill. If you have 5,000 SaaS-style accounts auto-generating reports, you probably need something with more dev resources behind it.
