## Before you start

You'll need:

- A **work email address** (recommended — see [Why work email matters](#why-work-email-matters) below).
- About **3 minutes**.

You will **not** need:

- A credit card. The trial runs 14 days, no card.
- Your team's invitation list. You can invite them later.
- Your client list. You'll set up your first client in the next step.

## The signup flow

<Placeholder type="screenshot" caption="The /signup page with email, name, and password fields visible" />

1. Go to [app.sendbriefs.com/signup](https://app.sendbriefs.com/signup).
2. Enter your email, your full name, and a password (12+ characters).
3. *Optional*: add your agency name now. You can fill or change it later.
4. Submit. Check your inbox for the verification email.
5. Click the verification link. It expires in 30 minutes — request a new one from the login screen if it's stale.
6. You're signed in.

> **Password policy.** We require 12+ characters and check it against the [Have I Been Pwned](https://haveibeenpwned.com/) breach database. If your password has shown up in a known breach, the signup form will reject it and ask for a different one. This is non-negotiable: a reused password protects nothing.

## Signing up with Google

If your workspace has Google sign-in enabled, you'll also see a **Continue with Google** button on the signup screen. Pick that to skip the password setup — your Google account becomes the login. You can later set a password in **Settings → Profile** if you want a fallback.

If you don't see the Google button, your workspace doesn't have it enabled. Email is the only option for now.

## Why work email matters

Two reasons:

- **Billing and account recovery** route through this email. A personal Gmail you'll lose access to when you change jobs is a bad anchor.
- **Team identification** is easier when colleagues share a domain. Future invitations and workspace-level controls expect a consistent email pattern.

If you signed up with a personal email by mistake, you can change it later from **Settings → Profile**, but it's faster to use the right one from the start.

## Verify your email

Until you click the verification link, your account can read its own settings but can't send briefs. The link lands you back in the app — pick up the onboarding wizard from there.

Didn't get the email? Check spam. If it's truly missing, request a new one from the login page — old links expire in 30 minutes anyway.

## Turn on two-factor authentication

Highly recommended. You can do it now or after the workspace setup:

1. Click your avatar / Profile in the sidebar and open **Profile → Security** (route: `/profile/security`).
2. Click **Enable two-factor authentication**.
3. Scan the QR code with your authenticator app (1Password, Authy, Google Authenticator, anything TOTP-compatible).
4. Enter the 6-digit code to confirm.
5. **Save the recovery codes somewhere safe.** If you lose your phone, these are how you get back in.

If your workspace owner has **enforced MFA** for the workspace, the system will route you to `/mfa-enroll` at next login and require you to enroll before you can do anything else. Same flow.
